Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical website project take?

Simple websites: 1-2 weeks. Complex web applications: 4-8 weeks. Timeline depends on scope, features, and revision rounds.

Do you provide hosting and domain services?

I can guide you in purchasing your own domain and hosting, or deploy on platforms like Firebase, Vercel, or Netlify. Hosting costs are separate from development fees.

Can you work with my existing website?

Yes! I can redesign, update, or add features to existing websites. I'll assess your current site and recommend the best approach.

What if I need changes after launch?

All projects include post-launch support. After the support period, I offer maintenance packages or hourly rates for updates.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! For larger projects, I offer milestone-based payments (e.g., 50% upfront, 50% on delivery). We'll discuss payment terms during the proposal stage.

Can you help with ongoing digital marketing?

Absolutely! I offer monthly retainer packages for ongoing campaign management, content creation, and optimization.
